Our Services

Sofa and Mattress cleaning

Furniture is the focal point of your home or office. It helps provide the design and atmosphere you desire, while creating a relaxing place for you to enjoy time with your family and guests. Over time these pieces get worn and faded due to regular wear and tear, but also due to spills and food that can cause stains. Our upholstery cleaning service makes your valuable furniture look new again, eliminating stains and refreshing the colors giving your upholstery the clean look and feel they deserve.

We clean all types of upholstered furniture including:

  • Sofas
  • Recliners and Ottomans
  • Dining Chairs
  • Wingback Chairs and Arm Chairs
  • Mattresses
  • Patio Furniture

With Every Service

Assessment

Our technicians will assess the carpeted area and/or upholstered material to determine the best method to employ.

Pre-Treatment

Our technicians will use a pH-balanced formula to loosen up the fibers in preparation for extraction.

Spot and Stain Treatment

Our technicians will use a concentrated solution to tackle the more stubborn and spotty areas. While we cannot guarantee removal of stains, we will try our best to remove or reduce the spots we come across.

Water Extraction (Steam cleaning)

Our technicians will use water, pressure and steam to penetrate deep within your fibers to extract dirt and harmful allergens. The most recommended method by carpet and upholstery manufacturers.

No Residue Carpet Cleaning

Restore your carpets and banish dirt allergens that are stuck deep inside with professional carpet cleaning from Toronto Steam n' Clean. We use the best cleaning products and equipments to ensure our carpet cleaning services are safe, reliable, and better than the rest. We use a combination of truck-mounted and portable solutions using the hot water extraction method commonly known as steam cleaning. Our process has been honed and perfected over the last two decades to ensure your carpets are left in the best possible solution.
