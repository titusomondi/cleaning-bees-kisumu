Welcome to Cleaning Bees Kisumu

Cleaning Bees Kisumu is a professional cleaning service company based in Kisumu, Kenya, offering a wide range of cleaning services for residential and commercial properties.
Our comprehensive range of services include sofa and mattress cleaning, general cleaning, fumigation, post-construction cleaning, and steam cleaning.
At Cleaning Bees Kisumu, we are committed to meeting the diverse cleaning needs for our customers with high quality and efficient cleaning solutions.
We take pride in having experienced and trained staff who use the best equipments in the industry.
Our focus on serving the local community has helped us build a strong reputation for providing exceptional cleaning services.
