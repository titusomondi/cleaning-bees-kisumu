Frequent Asked Questions

How often should I clean my sofa?

It is recommended to have your sofa professionally cleaned at least once a year. However, if you have children, pets, or if your sofa is frequently used, it may need to be cleaned more often. Regular cleaning helps maintain the appearance and hygiene of your sofa, extending its lifespan.

Client safety: What measures do we take?

Ensuring your safety and comfort is our top priority. All our technicians are full-time employees who have undergone thorough background checks. Additionally, they receive extensive professional training to handle a range of cleaning tasks and different types of upholstery and carpets. This rigorous process ensures that you can trust our team entirely.

What is included in the price?

Our pricing is straightforward and transparent. The total cost includes labor, cleaning agents, and the use of high-tech equipment. There are absolutely no hidden charges or surprise fees.

What services do you offer?

At Cleaning Bees Kisumu, we offer a comprehensive suite of cleaning services to cater to various needs. Our portfolio includes carpet cleaning, couch cleaning, mattress cleaning, rugs cleaning, chair cleaning, and leather furniture cleaning. Each of these services goes beyond just superficial cleaning. For instance, our couch cleaning methods not only tackle visible stains but also eliminate hidden bacteria and germs.
